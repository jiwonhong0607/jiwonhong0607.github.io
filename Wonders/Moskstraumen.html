The Moskstraumen in Norway is the strongest whirpool existing
One of the largest whirpools has a diameter of some 40–50 meters (130–160 ft) and induces surface water ripples up to 1 meter.
In the past the Mokstraumen was something to be feared as often fishing boats would dissapear by it.
It would often appear in old literature and mythology but nowadays it has become a sight of attraction for people visiting Norway.
