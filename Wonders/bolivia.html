The Salar de Uyuni is a salt flat located in Bolivia.
Measuring at 10,582 km, Salar de Uyuni is the biggest salt flat in the world.
At times the salt flat is covered in very clear water, making it the largest natural mirror in the world.
Estimated to have originated from the Andes mountain 30,000 to 40,000 years ago.
Today it is a very popular tourist attraction and thousands flock every year to visit.
